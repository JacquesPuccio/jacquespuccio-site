About Me

I analyze businesses.
Seriously.

I'm Jacques Puccio — a business analyst and consultant focused on one thing: understanding what's really happening inside your company.

I've worked across multiple industries, and what I've found is that most businesses have the same core problem — they're running on assumptions rather than facts. Nobody has sat down and looked carefully at the full picture.

That's what I do. I look at everything — operations, financials, team dynamics, customer journey, market positioning — and I give you a clear, honest assessment. No sugar-coating. No generic advice. Just specific findings about your business.
